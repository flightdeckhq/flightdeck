# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
Flightdeck community a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways
that contribute to an open, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Standard

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant][cc], version 2.1, as its code
of conduct. The full text — the standards of behavior, the enforcement
guidelines, and the scope — is published at the link below and is
incorporated here by reference.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported to the project maintainers through the repository's
GitHub page (open a private report via the **Security** tab, or contact a
maintainer directly). Reports are reviewed promptly and fairly, and the
maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
